Biography

Khadga Prasad (KP) Sharma Oli (born 22 February 1952) is a prominent Nepalese communist politician who has served multiple terms as Prime Minister of Nepal (2015–2016, 2018–2021, and 2024–2025). As chairman of the CPN (UML) since 2014, he is known for his nationalistic, pro-China stance, infrastructural focus, and a turbulent political career that included 14 years in prison.
